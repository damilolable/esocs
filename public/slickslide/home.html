THE ETERNAL SACRED ORDER OF THE CHERUBIM AND SERAPHIM CHURCH

Welcome to the official website of The Eternal Sacred Order of the Cherubim and Seraphim (ESOCS) Church. A church founded by Saint Moses Orimolade Tunolase (Ajagunmokadi) of the blessed memory for the salvation of mankind through the inspiration of the Holy Ghost in the year of our Lord, 1925 and registered under the Company Ordinance Act [Cap 316] in 1930.

VISION STATEMENT

To Represent And Establish The Kingdom Of God On On Earth Through Prayer And The Preaching Of The Gospel

MISSION STATEMENT

To Arouse The Practice Of A Spiritual Christian Life And Faith As Taught By Jesus Christ Himself To The African In Particular, And The World In General.

CORE VALUES

Love, Unshaken Faith, Holiness, Respect, Diligence And Sacrificial Service

ARTICLE OF FAITH

The church holds unshaken faith in the Holy Bible as the word of God; in salvation through Jesus Christ; and in the Unity of the Trinity. The church believes in the use of incense, purification by prayer and fasting and the resurrection of the dead
